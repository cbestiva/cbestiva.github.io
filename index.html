About Me...

I'm a born and raised San Franciscan and former accountant who gained an interest in web development. After completing an HTML and CSS evening course at AcademyX I became hooked and wanted to learn more code. I began learning Ruby on my own from free online tutorials and attended a couple ROR and JavaScript meetups in the city. At the end of 2013, I got accepted to General Assembly's Web Development Immersive 12-week program. I am proficient in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript/JQuery. I am also experienced in PostgreSQL, Git, Github, AJAX, JSON, Bootstrap, Backbone.js, Angular.js, and Heroku. Throughout my time at GA, I've found that I am most passionate about front-end development, simple clean designs, and building projects with a mobile first mentality.

I am a dog owner of 2 and a loyal bay area sports team fanatic. Outside of programming, I enjoy playing golf and spending time with family and friends in So Cal and Nor Cal.
